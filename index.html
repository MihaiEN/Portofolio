Professional Profile

Clear mathematics, carefully structured.

I teach mathematics by making complex ideas precise, structured and accessible. My work spans classroom teaching, online tuition, curriculum planning, assessment writing and examiner-quality review.

My practice is built around clear explanation, carefully chosen examples and purposeful assessment. I use student responses and progress information to identify misconceptions, improve feedback and plan targeted support.

Digital tools and responsible AI are used selectively to support planning, resource development and reflection. They do not replace subject knowledge, professional judgement or direct engagement with students.
