Approach

How I approach mathematics teaching

These principles describe the way I plan, teach, tutor and give feedback.

01

Clarity before speed

I prioritise clear explanations, clean examples and careful modelling before asking students to work independently.

02

Practice with purpose

I use sequenced tasks to move students from basic fluency towards reasoning and unfamiliar exam-style problems.

03

Feedback that leads somewhere

I aim to give feedback that is specific, actionable and useful for the student’s next attempt.
