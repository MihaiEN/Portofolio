Approach

How I approach mathematics teaching

A concise explanation of the professional principles behind the evidence.

01

Clarity before speed

I prioritise precise explanations, clean examples and careful modelling before asking students to work independently.

02

Practice with purpose

I use carefully sequenced tasks so students move from fluency to reasoning and then to unfamiliar exam-style problems.

03

Feedback that improves the next attempt

I aim to give feedback that is specific, actionable and linked to the mathematical thinking students need to develop.
