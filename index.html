Public Profile

Clear teaching, careful assessment and structured curriculum thinking.

I work across mathematics, STEM education and assessment. My teaching experience includes classroom teaching, online provision, tutoring and international learner support, with a focus on making demanding ideas clear, precise and usable.

My practice is shaped by strong subject knowledge, careful sequencing and purposeful assessment. I use student responses, quiz outcomes and progress information to identify misconceptions, refine explanations and plan the next stage of support.
