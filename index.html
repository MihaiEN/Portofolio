Teaching Approach

How I teach mathematics

My teaching approach is based on clarity, structure and confidence. I believe students learn mathematics best when ideas are broken down carefully, modelled clearly, practised deliberately and revisited over time.

I aim to help students understand not only how to complete a method, but why it works, how it connects to previous learning and how it can be applied in unfamiliar exam-style problems.

Clarity

Clear explanations, clean examples and careful modelling.

Challenge

Tasks that move students from fluency to reasoning.

Feedback

Precise feedback that helps students improve their next attempt.
